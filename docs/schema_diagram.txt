SCHEMA CHARACTERISTICS:
• Star Schema Design - Optimal for analytical queries
• 1 Fact Table (SalesFact) - Contains business measures
• 4 Dimension Tables - Customer, Product, Time, Location
• Foreign Key Relationships - Maintains referential integrity
• Strategic Indexing - Optimizes query performance
• Denormalized Structure - Fast aggregations and analysis

BUSINESS BENEFITS:
• Fast analytical query performance
• Simple and intuitive structure
• Easy maintenance and modification
• Supports complex business intelligence
• Scalable for growing data volumes
• Business-user friendly queries

DESIGN RATIONALE:
• Chosen over normalized schemas for analytical performance
• Balances simplicity with functionality
• Follows data warehousing best practices
• Enables self-service business intelligence
• Supports retail business requirements
